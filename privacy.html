Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Lina ("we", "our", "us") is operated by Solacium Ventures Ltd. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

What we collect

We keep data collection to an absolute minimum.

Information you provide voluntarily

  • Email address: If you choose to join our waitlist or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is the only personal information we collect through this website.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not use analytics tools on this website
  • We do not track your browsing behavior
  • We do not collect device information, IP addresses, or location data through this website
  • We do not use third-party tracking pixels

How we use your email

If you provide your email address through our waitlist form, we use it solely to:

  • Notify you when the Lina app is available for download
  • Send occasional updates about Lina (no more than once per month)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: To process waitlist form submissions. When you submit your email, it is processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • GitHub Pages: To host this website. GitHub may collect basic server logs. Their privacy statement is available at docs.github.com.
  • Google Fonts: To serve typefaces used on this website. Google may collect basic usage data when fonts are loaded. Their privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (limited to your email address, if provided)
  • Delete your email from our waitlist at any time
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you request its removal, or until it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected (e.g., after the app launches and you have been notified).

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
